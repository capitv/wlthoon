Berathoon Roadmap

Our Journey

The Berathoon NFT Collection has a clear path forward, with strategic milestones designed to deliver increasing value to our community. Our roadmap outlines our vision for the collection and the ecosystem we're building.

1
Completed

Community Building

Establishing our community and brand presence. We've successfully built a strong following on Twitter and Discord, with over 10,000 members joining our community channels. Our art direction has been finalized and the whitelist process is underway.

2
In Progress

Pre-mint Phase

Final preparations for our mint on April 9, 2025. We're conducting whitelist verification, finalizing partnerships with key Berachain projects, and implementing our smart contracts. Marketing efforts are being amplified across all channels.

3
Upcoming

Mint & Secondary Market

April 9-11, 2025. Our phased mint will begin with GTD allocations for whitelisted addresses, followed by FCFS and community allocations. Secondary market listings will be established on major NFT marketplaces with royalties supporting the community treasury.

4
Upcoming

DAO & Utility Launch

Q2 2025. Implementation of our governance structure and utility mechanisms. Holders will gain access to the BeraDAO, with voting rights proportional to their holdings. Honey Token will be launched with an initial airdrop to NFT holders.

5
Future

Ecosystem Integration

Q3-Q4 2025. Deeper integration with the Berachain ecosystem through partnerships and collaborative projects. Holders will gain privileged access to partner projects, exclusive Bear Den metaverse experiences, and community-led initiatives.

6
Future

Expansion & Evolution

2026 and beyond. Long-term growth of the Berathoon ecosystem with companion collections, expanded gameplay elements, exclusive merchandise, IRL events, and community-driven development. Our vision is to establish Berathoon as a cornerstone of the Berachain ecosystem.

The Path Forward

Our ultimate vision extends beyond the roadmap. We are committed to growing with the Berachain ecosystem, creating opportunities for our community, and pushing the boundaries of what NFTs can achieve. As the ecosystem evolves, so too will Berathoon.
